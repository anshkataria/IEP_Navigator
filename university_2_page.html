About the University

The University of Toledo (UToledo) is a comprehensive public research university located in Toledo, Ohio. Renowned for its commitment to innovation and student success, UToledo offers a dynamic academic environment with state-of-the-art facilities.

Academic Offerings

Available Courses

  • Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Bachelor of Environmental Science
  • Master of Computer Science

Eligibility Criteria

  • High school diploma or equivalent with a GPA of 2.5 or higher.
  • English proficiency: IELTS (6.0) or TOEFL (80).
  • Subject prerequisites for technical programs.

Accommodation Options

UToledo provides various housing options for students to meet diverse preferences and budgets:

  • On-campus dormitories with modern amenities and dining plans.
  • Private apartments near campus.
  • Shared housing for international students to foster community living.
