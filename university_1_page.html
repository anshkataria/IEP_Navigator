About the University

The University of Queensland (UQ) is a globally renowned institution known for its cutting-edge research, world-class faculty, and outstanding academic programs. Located in Brisbane, Australia, UQ fosters innovation and nurtures leaders for the future.

Academic Offerings

Available Courses

  • Bachelor of Engineering (Honours)
  • Master of Data Science
  • Bachelor of Information Technology
  • Master of Civil Engineering

Eligibility Criteria

  • High school graduation with a minimum GPA of 3.0.
  • English proficiency: IELTS (6.5) or TOEFL (87).
  • Completion of subject prerequisites for specific programs.

Accommodation Options

Students at UQ have access to a range of housing options to suit different lifestyles and budgets:

  • On-campus residential colleges with meal plans and community events.
  • Affordable off-campus apartments in Brisbane city.
  • Homestay options with local families for a cultural experience.
