About the University

The University of New South Wales (UNSW) is one of Australia’s leading research and teaching universities. Located in Sydney, UNSW is committed to academic excellence and innovation, empowering students to achieve global impact.

Academic Offerings

Available Courses

  • Bachelor of Software Engineering
  • Master of Artificial Intelligence
  • Bachelor of Biomedical Engineering
  • Master of Environmental Management

Eligibility Criteria

  • High school diploma with excellent grades (GPA 3.5+).
  • English proficiency: IELTS (7.0) or TOEFL (94).
  • Specific subject requirements for certain programs.

Accommodation Options

UNSW offers a variety of accommodation options to cater to student needs:

  • On-campus colleges with inclusive facilities.
  • Off-campus housing in Sydney’s vibrant neighborhoods.
  • Short-term accommodation for international students.
