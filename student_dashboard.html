What is the 2+2 IEP?

The International Engineering Program (IEP) is an exciting opportunity for students to complete the first two years of their engineering education at Thapar Institute and the remaining two years at a partner university abroad. This unique program is designed to provide global exposure and academic excellence, helping students grow into well-rounded professionals.
